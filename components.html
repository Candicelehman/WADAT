ABOUT US

WADAT (pronounced “where it at”) is a Florida based company started by seasoned technology pioneers that plan to bring affordable, reliable, geo-tracking to the mainstream. Our competitors use 2G, 3G, 4G and Satellite based networks that require long commitments, are difficult to cancel and cost $9-$100 per month per device.

WADAT currently maintains a network operating in the Sarasota/Bradenton area of Florida, and pland plans to expand nationally. Please contact us if you have a large-scale need restricted to a metopolitan area. We may be able to deploy our network in your area sooner!
